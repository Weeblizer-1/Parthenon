The Parthenon

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎The model I created represents the remains of the Parthenon, a symbol of Greek architectural achievement. The Greeks used pillars and arches in their designs, which are featured in my model. These elements are still used today for both structural support and aesthetic appeal. Greek architecture has greatly influenced much of the architecture we see today. It is as Ralph Waldo Emerson said, "Earth proudly wears the Parthenon as the best gem upon her zone."

Vocab:

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎Architecture - the art or practice of designing and constructing buildings.

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎Pillar - a tall vertical structure of stone, wood, or metal, used as a support for a building, or as an ornament or monument.

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ Arch - a curved symmetrical structure spanning an opening and typically supporting the weight of a bridge, roof, or wall above it.
